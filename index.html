Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 29, 2025

At Airplane Wiki, we respect your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

  • Email Addresses: When you submit an aircraft through our form, we may collect your email address. This helps us verify submissions and communicate if needed.
  • Reading Time: We track how long you view individual aircraft images to better understand user engagement and improve the platform.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To log aircraft submissions
  • To measure which aircraft are most viewed or popular
  • To improve website features and user experience
  • To respond to questions or concerns you may raise

3. Data Storage and Protection

Your data is securely stored using trusted tools provided by Google, such as Google Forms and Google Sheets. Only authorized administrators have access to this data.

We are committed to protecting your information and will do our best to keep it safe using reliable technologies and services from reputable providers like Google.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties. We may share general statistics (e.g., popular aircraft types) that do not identify individual users.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies or third-party advertising trackers. However, we do use a basic timer on the website to measure how long users view specific aircraft content.

6. Your Choices and Rights

You may request to review, update, or delete your personal data by contacting us at your@email.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. When we make changes, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us at your@email.com.
